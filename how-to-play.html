Q-Dara Field Manual

How to Play

Arcane Glitch is a tactical dice-crafting run. Explore realm maps, gather Sigils, build dice faces, cast Programs, collect Rift Threads, and close the Rift before the room drains too many turns from your Cycle reward.

Goal

Each combat level has Rift Threads hidden across the hex map. Collect every Rift Thread, then move onto the Rift Hex to close the level. Enemies do not have to be defeated, but clearing them makes the room safer.

Turns

Your turn uses a shared Actions pool. Movement, Sigil-Casting, changing Programs, and Crafting all spend Actions. Ending the turn lets Glitchspawn move, attack, spawn, or lose stun effects.

Movement

Move along connected hexes. Walls block movement and most attacks unless a Program says otherwise. Enemy hexes cannot be entered unless a special Program destroys or bypasses the enemy.

Sigil-Casting

Sigil-Cast rolls your equipped dice during one Action phase. Executions are the number of roll attempts inside that Sigil-Cast. Spend rolled Sigils to activate Programs, or reroll unspent dice while Executions remain.

Dice Crafting

Dice faces hold Sigils. Blank faces roll blank. Pips can be crafted onto faces for extra effects, such as gaining Cycles or making a face more likely to roll.

Programs

Programs are not tied to a single element. They are cast by matching their required Sigil symbols. Cooldowns lock used Programs for later turns, and some rare Programs can only be used once per level.

Room Flow

  1. Enter a level from the World Map or visit a one-time Market node.
  2. Explore by moving through visible and newly revealed hexes.
  3. Collect Rift Threads, Sigils, Artifacts, Cache rewards, Cycles, Havens, and Power Hubs.
  4. Cast Programs by rolling dice and spending the matching Sigils shown in the Program requirement.
  5. Close the Rift after all Rift Threads are collected.

Rewards

  • Sigils: New symbols for crafting onto blank die faces.
  • Programs: Added through Cache rewards and equipped up to your CACHE stat.
  • Artifacts: Permanent boosts, one-use tools, Sigil-Glyphs, and craftable pips.
  • Cycles: Currency earned from enemies, pickups, and fast level completion.
  • XP: Defeating Glitchspawn levels you up and grants new blank dice.
  • Markets: One-time realm shops where Cycles buy Artifacts and Sigil-Glyphs.

Combat Tips

  • Physical Damage is reduced by Physical Defense; Arcane Damage is reduced by Arcane Defense.
  • Walls block enemy attacks and most Program damage unless a Program explicitly bypasses walls.
  • BLINK-style movement is important for islands and one-hex gaps in higher-tier rooms.
  • Blank rolls do not end your turn by themselves. Use remaining Actions or Executions deliberately.
